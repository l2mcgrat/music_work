Response Highlights

Treat power as something that exists in the vacuum between Sin and Virtue. Pride wants power for self-exaltation; Humility yields power to serve. The middle ground is realizing power itself is completely neutral—it's a magnifying glass that just shows who you really are.

Self-exaltation is the act of elevating oneself in status, importance, or power, often characterized by pride, arrogance, and the pursuit of personal glory.

This is where you contrast the binary of your first two album sections.

The Misuse (Sin Alignment): Using power to isolate yourself, build walls, crush competition (Wrath/Pride), or hoard resources (Greed).

The Right Use (Virtue Alignment): Power as a shield, a platform to lift others, or a tool for order (Justice/Fortitude).

Angle for the Lyrics: Use heavy juxtaposition. Talk about weaponizing a position versus stabilizing a community.
